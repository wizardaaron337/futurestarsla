Privacy Policy
How we handle your data

1. Information We Collect

Future Stars Sports Co operates entirely in your browser. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to external servers. All data (trip plans, inventory counts, tournament information) is stored locally on your device using browser storage.

2. Local Storage

We use browser localStorage and sessionStorage to save:

  • Your login session (role, name, PIN verification)
  • Trip plans and folder organization
  • Jersey packing lists and inventory tracking
  • User preferences and settings

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

3. No Cookies or Tracking

We do not use cookies, tracking pixels, analytics scripts, or any third-party tracking technologies. Your browsing activity on this site is completely private.

4. GitHub Pages Hosting

This website is hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub may collect basic server logs (IP address, browser type, access times) as part of their standard hosting service. We do not have access to or control over these logs. See GitHub's Privacy Statement for details.

5. Third-Party Links

Our site may contain links to external websites (Booking.com, Google Maps, etc.) for trip planning convenience. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites.

6. Data Security

Since all data is stored locally in your browser:

  • Clearing browser data will erase all saved trips and plans
  • Using incognito/private mode will not preserve data between sessions
  • Your data is as secure as your device

7. Children's Privacy

This site is intended for business use by Future Stars crew members. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at support@futurestarsla.com.
