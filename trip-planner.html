💡 PJ'S TRAVEL TIPS
  • Book hotels 2+ weeks out for better rates. Last minute = $$$.
  • Airport car rentals usually cheaper than downtown.
  • Check if hotel has free parking before booking.
  • Marriott/Hilton chains = consistent quality for crew.
  • Screenshot your confirmations — cell service dies at fields.
  • Always get a confirmation number. "I called" doesn't count.
